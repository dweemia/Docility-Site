THE SCENERY — filmstrip photos
==============================

The "The scenery" section is a full-bleed horizontal filmstrip of your photos —
the places behind the music and the source of the album art. The list lives in
the GALLERY array near the top of ../../main.js (src / alt / caption).

Photos
- Drop LANDSCAPE photos here named to match the array: 01.webp ... 06.webp
  (add or remove entries in GALLERY to change the count).
- Size them for the web: ~1600px on the long edge, WebP, ideally <300 KB each.
- Frames are shown 4:3; images are cropped to fill, so centre the subject.
- A missing photo shows a labelled placeholder instead of breaking the strip.

Captions
- Each `caption` overlays the bottom of the photo — a good place for the
  location and which track/album it inspired.
